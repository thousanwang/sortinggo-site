Support & Contact

What We Can Help With

If you run into any issues while using SortingGo, we're happy to help, including:

  • App usage questions and how-tos
  • Subscription status issues or features not unlocking
  • Excel / CSV import or export issues
  • Voice readout and headset media-button control issues
  • Feature suggestions and feedback
  • Bug reports

Contact Us

Please send your questions by email to:

Email: thousan.tw@gmail.com

When Reporting an Issue, Please Include

To help us diagnose and resolve issues faster, please include as much of the following as possible:

  • Phone model (e.g., Samsung Galaxy S24, Pixel 8)
  • Android version (found under Settings → About phone)
  • App version (found in the app's Settings page or on Google Play)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots of any error messages (very helpful if available)

The more detail you provide, the faster we can respond. Thank you for your cooperation.

Subscription Management

If your question is related to subscription charges, renewals, refunds, or cancellations, please manage these through your Google Play account or contact Google Play support. The developer cannot process Google Play payments or refunds on your behalf.
