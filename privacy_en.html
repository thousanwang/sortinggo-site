Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-14

Introduction

Thank you for using SortingGo. This Privacy Policy explains how this app handles your data. Please read it carefully — by continuing to use this app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Data Collection

SortingGo is a local, offline-first tool. Other than information required for Google Play subscription services, this app does not actively collect, transmit, or store any personal data on developer servers.

We do not actively collect your name, email address, phone number, home address, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. Local Data Storage

All data generated while you use SortingGo is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Imported order data
  • Batch and sorting results
  • Routing sequence settings
  • App settings and preferences

The developer cannot access the order and sorting data stored on your device. This data remains entirely on your phone or tablet and is never uploaded to any external server.

3. Google Play Subscriptions

SortingGo offers subscriptions through Google Play. Details are as follows:

  • Payments are processed entirely by Google Play; neither this app nor the developer ever receives your credit card or bank account information.
  • Neither this app nor the developer ever receives your Google account password.
  • Your subscription status may be returned to the app by Google Play to determine whether corresponding features are unlocked — this is required for normal app functionality.
  • Google Play's own privacy policy applies separately; please refer to Google's official documentation.

4. Permissions

SortingGo may request the following device permissions, used only when the corresponding feature is enabled:

  • File access: used to import Excel (.xlsx) or CSV order data, and to export sorting results.
  • Microphone: used for voice readout and voice command features (if enabled by the user).
  • Bluetooth / media control: used for headset media-button control, allowing hands-free operation.
  • Notifications: used to show status for the background media-button service (if applicable).

None of these permissions are used to collect personal data, and none are activated without your consent.

5. Data Sharing

This app does not sell, rent, or otherwise share user data with third parties. We do not run ads, use tracking identifiers, or integrate any third-party analytics SDKs.

6. Deleting Your Data

You can manage or remove your data in the following ways:

  • Use the in-app data-clearing feature to delete locally stored order and sorting records.
  • Uninstall SortingGo, which automatically removes all local app data stored on your device.
  • To manage your subscription records, please use your Google Play account.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: thousan.tw@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

As SortingGo's features continue to evolve, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "last updated" date on this page. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically for the latest information.
