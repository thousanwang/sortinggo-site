Fast, organized order allocation, preparation, picking and sorting — right on your phone

About SortingGo

SortingGo is a mobile-first, offline-first assistant for order allocation, preparation, picking and sorting. Whether you run a small business, organize group buys, operate a food wholesale business, handle fresh produce delivery, or sell on e-commerce platforms, SortingGo helps you complete pre-shipment sorting quickly and systematically, right from your phone.

Key Features

  • Import orders from Excel / CSV for fast data setup
  • Lite / Std import formats for flexible scenarios
  • Putting and Picking sorting modes
  • Voice readout for hands-free, faster operation
  • Headset media-button control, no need to touch the screen
  • Routing sequence support for optimized picking paths based on your actual layout
  • Fully offline — no internet connection required
  • Great fit for small businesses, group-buy organizers, wholesalers, fresh produce delivery, and e-commerce shipping
